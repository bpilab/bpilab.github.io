Oct 19, 2022 - Royal Society report on Geological Carbon Storage

The Royal Society has recently released a new report on geological carbon storage: storing CO2 kilometres below the Earth’s surface to remove it from the atmosphere for millenia.

The report explores:

  • The challenges of this storage and the variability between sites
  • Safety measures to prevent CO2 escape
  • The need for future research and policy developments
