Carbon Storage

The BPI has several projects exploring aspects of carbon capture and storage, particularly the fluid dynamics of geological carbon storage (Prof Jerome Neufeld; Prof Andy Woods). Several PhD students and post-doctoral researchers study the following general problems:

  • The use of tracers to monitor CO2 migration through porous rocks
  • Modelling the seismic reflection associated with a moving CO2 plume
  • Modelling the elastic response in a reservoir as CO2 is injected into a well
  • Assessing the storage capacity of CO2 in porous rocks
  • Modelling the dynamics of CO2 leakage from subsurface reservoirs
