Natural Environment

Research into the natural environment includes research directed at modelling ice sheet dynamics (Dr Jerome Neufeld) and glacier melting into fjords (Prof Andy Woods). There are also models of ocean mixing processes, using numerical and experimental approaches (Prof Colm Caulfield and Prof Andy Woods).

We also have an active programme exploring the dynamics of particle laden flows, with relevance to submarine turbidite flows and also dust storms. This includes projects such as:

  • Cracks in drying red blood cells
  • Colloidal encapsulation
