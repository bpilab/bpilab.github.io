Sep 18, 2023 - Second Cambridge "Flows in the Environment" Seminar

The second Cambridge “Flows in the Environment” seminar will be held at the Institute for Energy and Environmental Flows on 28th September 2023

All are welcome; no registration is needed.

Programme

10:00 - Andy Woods - Bubble plumes and Lake Nyos

10:20 - Andy Archibald - Environmental consequences of a future hydrogen economy

10:45 - Ali Mashayek - Self-optimisation of mixing in ocean MOC

11:10 - Coffee

11:30 - Adam Butler - Sub-surface flows and ground deformation

11:55 - Clive Oppenheimer - Lava Lake Systems

12:20 - Daria Frank - Virtual barriers for single-sided ventilation and beyond

12:45 - Lunch

13:15 - Nico Petropoulos - Density field structures across scales and Prandtl number effects in stably stratified turbulent flows

13:50 - Alex Routh - Making rubber gloves

14:15 - Hassan Sheikh - Impact of hedges on dispersal of traffic pollution

14:50 - Marie Edmonds - Volcanic gas plume studies using unoccupied aerial vehicles

15:15 - Tea and close
